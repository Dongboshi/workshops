## Frameworks

> A framework is a collection of stylesheets, javascripts and layouts that control the style and appearance of a slide deck.

Here is an [example](index.Rmd)

    ---
    title: Frameworks
    framework: revealjs
    mode: selfcontained
    ---
     
    ## Slide 1
     
    Some content
     
    --- 
     
    ## Slide 2
     
    Some content
